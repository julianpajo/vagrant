Services.

Displacement

A geoinformation service designed specifically to detect and report earth’s surface movements, aimed at monitoring areas subject to landslides and subsidence, and the stability of infrastructures, as buildings, roads and railways.

Marine

A geoinformation service designed for the monitoring of coastal seawaters quality, in order to meet the environmental reporting requirements of local governments in compliance with the EU Directive on "Marine Strategy".

Burned area

A geoinformation service designed to support burned area monitoring, through burned area mapping and detection of illegal land use changes.

Urban
